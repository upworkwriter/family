2. What is the best way to keep track of medication schedules and dosages?
Use a pill organizer or medication reminder app, keep a written log of doses taken, and set alarms or reminders on your phone.
3. How do I handle expired or unused medications safely?
Dispose of expired or unused medications through a drug take-back program, mix them with undesirable substances like cat litter before throwing them out, and avoid flushing them down the toilet.
4. What should I do if I experience side effects or interactions from my medications?
Contact your doctor immediately if you experience any unexpected side effects or interactions between medications, document any symptoms you notice, and seek medical attention if necessary.
